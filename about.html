ABOUT ADDISON VIENER

UI/UX Graphic Designer, Artist & Entrepreneur

Hello, my name is Addison Viener. I'm a graduating senior from the University of Michigan with a dual degree in User Experience and Art & Design with a Minor in Entrepreneurship looking for front-end work as a UI/UX Designer, Researcher, and Developer. My passion is in helping companies to discover, design, test, validate, and develop innovative products that create unique experiences for customers and strengthen brands.
